Capital Bank is an Indian multinational, private sector banking and financial services statutory body headquartered in Landran, Mohali. CAPITAL BANK is ranked 500th in the Fortune Global 500 list of the world's biggest corporations of 2020. A nationalised bank, it is the largest in India with a 24% market share by assets and a 21% share of the total loan and deposits market. The bank descends from the Bank of Landran, founded in 1806 via the CPI Bank, making it the oldest commercial bank in the Indian subcontinent.
